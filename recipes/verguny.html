Verguny(Ukrainian Brushwood Cookies)



Here are the verguny ingredients you will need to make these tasty cookies:


  • Buttermilk
  • All-purpose flour
  • Brown sugar
  • Baking soda
  • Vegetable oil
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Powdered sugar

Here are the steps for this verguny

  1. Mix the Dry Ingredients
  2. Add the Wet Ingredients
  3. Rest the Batter
  4. Heat the Oil
  5. Fry the Verguny
  6. Drain and Cool
  7. Serve
  8. Enjoy
