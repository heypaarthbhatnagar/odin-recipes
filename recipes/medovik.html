Medovik



To make this honey layer cake, here are the ingredients that you will need for the cake:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Runny honey
  • Eggs
  • Egg whites
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking soda
  • Baking powder
  • Butter

Here are the steps for this medovik

  1. Prepare the Cake Batter
  2. Bake the Cake Layers
  3. Prepare the Filling
  4. Assemble the Cake
  5. Decorate
  6. Chill and Serve
